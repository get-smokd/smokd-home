FAQ

How does SMOKD help me quit? ✛

SMOKD combines habit-breaking science, real-time motivation, and progress tracking to keep you on track every day.

Can I quit privately? ✛

Yes. You can keep your journey completely private or connect with others for extra motivation.

Does SMOKD work for all types of smokers? ✛

Absolutely — whether you smoke daily, socially, or have recently quit and want to stay smoke-free.

What if I relapse? ✛

SMOKD helps you reset without judgment, learn from the moment, and get back on track.

Is my data safe? ✛

Yes. We don’t sell your data, and everything stays private to your device unless you choose to share it.

Do I need to create an account? ✛

No. You can start tracking right away and sign in later if you want to sync.

Is SMOKD a medical treatment? ✛

No. SMOKD is a self-help tool designed to support your quit journey. It’s not a substitute for medical advice.

6. How heart and lung health improvements are determined?

Our app does not collect or access any personal health data (such as heart rate or lung tests). The displayed percentages are educational estimates calculated locally on the device using only the user’s self-entered quit date.

These estimates follow publicly available medical research from trusted sources such as the World Health Organization (WHO), Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC), and American Heart Association (AHA). The app maps the time since quitting to well-documented recovery milestones (e.g., improved circulation after 2 weeks, reduced heart disease risk after 1 year) and interpolates between them for a smooth percentage increase.
